Since the product is chart-based, analytical, and precise, we thought it could use a bit of imperfect human touch. So we added a series of strokes, arrows, and scribbles to emphasize the most important aspects of our messages and images, and to balance logic with spontaneity.
The color palette needed to be flexible and integrate multiple colors to meet data visualization needs. We developed a high-contrast system that uses over 20 colors for the charts in the hierarchy and can display up to 500 data points.

The folks at Ternary were thrilled with the end result, especially their development team, who could now easily integrate our designs into their development process.
